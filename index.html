About

Welcome to Basecrusher.com , this is a tool that clash of clans players can use when they are looking for a new strategy to learn and use. The site has a wide array of strategies for different townhalls in its repository, making it so everyone can find a suitable strategy for the base of their choice.

Basecrusher is community driven and heavily involved with the Zen Dynasty clan family and contains homebrewed attacks as well.

We feature all kinds of attacks and everyone can find something that fits their skill set, we've got everything.
